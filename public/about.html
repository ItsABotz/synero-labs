Why Synero Labs?

Synero Labs exists to unlock friction-free access to AI innovation. By putting model weights, datasets, and API endpoints on-chain, we remove gatekeepers and let builders compose intelligence like Lego bricks.

Core Principles

  • Open by default. All listings are transparently stored on Solana.
  • Creator-first. Royalties go straight to contributors every time an asset is deployed.
  • Speed. Solana’s 400 ms block times keep inference calls snappy.
  • Composable. Use any model as a building block in your own dApps.

How it Works

  1. Creators mint a ModelNFT containing metadata & license.
  2. Datasets are chunked, IPFS-pinned, and hashed into a Merkle tree.
  3. API endpoints are wrapped in a lightweight proxy that verifies payment in $SYN.
  4. Consumers pay once to unlock, or stream-pay per inference call.

Every interaction settles on-chain, providing an auditable record of provenance, usage, and revenue distribution.
